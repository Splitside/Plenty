Trade

You may know our pies from the local farmers’ markets or one of the food fairs around the country at which we have a presence. However, the majority of the pies we bake each week are destined for pubs, delicatessens, farm shops, village/community shops, cafes, garden centres, golf clubs and restaurants.

Perfect for pubs!

Our pies are particularly successful in pubs and we’re adding more to our customer base each week.

So what makes our pies work so well for a pub?
  • Put simply, a top quality pie cannot fail in a pub – year round (our busiest month for two years running has been August!)
  • Our seasonal range of fillings includes cream-based fillings for the warmer months, which can be served cold with salad or summery veg. You’ll find, as we do, that the shin of beef remains popular whatever the weather
  • Versatility – our regularly changing recipes provide your chefs with a ready-made ‘special’ of the day, served hot or cold. Every week, you have at least six fillings to choose from
  • A perfect ready-made vegetarian option
  • Your chef and his/her team no longer need to spend hours making a single filling pie (we know just how time consuming they are to make!) but instead focus on the rest of the menu
  • Creating a traditional or an innovative meal with a pie as the centrepiece is both simple, quick to prepare and great value
  • We know how to sell pies – it’s our business! We offer advice on what to serve with them. We can help you into an exciting world beyond pie and mash...
  • We produce an exclusive gourmet range for those proprietors whose customers expect something truly different. Current fillings from this range include partridge in buttermilk & proscecco and ox cheek in Madeira with shallots and pickled walnuts
  • Since they freeze perfectly, there’s no wastage
  • Your choice – some pubs use our branding, including the National Pie Awards ‘champion class’ logo, others prefer them to be ‘anonymous’.
